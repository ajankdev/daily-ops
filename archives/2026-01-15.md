### Palo Alto Networks automates customer intelligence document creation with agentic design
**Source:** Google Cloud (General)

> **Category:** [AI_INFRA]
> **Summary:** Palo Alto Networks has developed an AI agent using Google Cloud's Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Vertex AI to automate the creation of customer intelligence documents. The agent is deployed on Vertex AI Agent Engine and utilizes various GCP resources such as Vertex AI RAG Engine, Vertex AI Discovery Engine Search, and Cloud Pub/Sub. The solution involves a seven-step process, including metadata retrieval, question preparation, parallel processing, and answer synthesis using Gemini models.
> **Impact:** The automation of customer intelligence document creation has significantly reduced the time required for this task, improved consistency and quality, and enhanced accuracy. It has also allowed expert employees to refocus on high-value activities, driving operational efficiency and strategic re-focus of personnel. The use of managed cloud services and open-source frameworks has solved complex business challenges, demonstrating a practical application of agentic AI in the enterprise.
