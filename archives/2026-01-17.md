### How Palo Alto Networks built a multi tenant scalable Unified Data Platform
**Source:** Google Cloud (General)

> **Category:** [GCP_K8S_CORE]
> **Summary:** Palo Alto Networks transformed their data processing landscape by partnering with Google Cloud to build a unified multi-tenant platform powered by Dataflow, Pub/Sub, and BigQuery.
